OUR STORY

From a Simple Start to your Favorite Spot.

Yas Milktea House started as a small local spot with one goal: to serve drinks and snacks that bring people
together. What began as a simple idea slowly grew into a favorite hangout for friends, students, and families.

With a passion for creating refreshing flavors and satisfying bites, Yas Milktea House continues to serve
moments of joy in every cup, making each visit a little sweeter and more memorable.
