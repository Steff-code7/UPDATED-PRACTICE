PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: May 2026

At YAS Milktea & Foodies, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our ordering services in accordance with the Data Privacy Act.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Identification: We collect information such as your full name, mobile number, and email address to create your account and communicate order status.

Delivery Details: To fulfill your orders, we collect precise physical addresses and landmark information to ensure our riders can locate you.

Technical Data: When you access our site, we may automatically collect data such as your IP address, browser type, and device information to improve our website’s performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Order Fulfillment: Your data is primarily used to process transactions, prepare your food and drinks, and coordinate delivery to your location.

Customer Support: We use your contact information to respond to inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide updates regarding any changes to your order.

Service Optimization: Aggregated, non-identifying data is analyzed to understand customer preferences and improve our menu offerings and website user interface.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Service Providers: We share necessary data with trusted partners, such as payment gateways and delivery riders, solely to complete your transaction.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or to protect the safety and rights of our business.

No Data Selling: YAS Milktea & Foodies does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers for their own promotional purposes.

4. Data Security

Protection Measures: We implement various technical and organizational security measures, including encryption, to protect your data from unauthorized access or alteration.

User Responsibility: While we strive to protect your data, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password and login credentials.

Breach Notification: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we are committed to notifying affected users in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

5. Data Retention

Active Accounts: We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services.

Transaction History: Records of your purchases are kept for a limited period to comply with tax regulations and business auditing requirements.

Deletion Requests: Users may request the deletion of their personal data, which we will honor unless we are legally required to retain specific information.

6. Use of Cookies

Enhanced Experience: Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences, such as your preferred delivery area or items saved in your cart.

Analytics: We use cookies to track website traffic patterns, which helps us understand which pages are most popular among our customers.

User Control: You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of the ordering system.

7. Third-Party Links

External Websites: Our site may contain links to social media pages or payment platforms that are not operated by us.

Independent Policies: We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites you choose to visit.

Due Diligence: We encourage users to read the privacy policies of any external website they interact with through our platform.

8. Children’s Privacy

Age Appropriateness: Our services are intended for a general audience and are not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.

Parental Consent: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental guidance and consent.

Data Removal: If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor without verification, we will take steps to remove that information immediately.

9. Your Rights and Contact Information

Access and Correction: You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you and request corrections to any inaccuracies.

Policy Updates: YAS Milktea & Foodies reserves the right to update this policy at any time; changes will be posted on this page with an updated timestamp.

Contact Us: For any questions regarding your privacy, you may contact our Data Privacy Officer through our official Facebook page or provided support email.
